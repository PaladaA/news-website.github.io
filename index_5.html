Bolton boxer has signed a multi-fight deal with Eddie Hearn's Matchroom, after a spell in the Australian jungle

Former unified light-welterweight ruler Khan is determined to get back on the road to world title glory in his first fight of a three-fight deal with Matchroom Boxing.

He has not fought in the UK since beating Julio Diaz in Sheffield back in 2013.

And after a spell appearing on I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out Of Here! last year, where he endured horror trials in the Australian jungle,the boxer is ready to lace up the gloves once again.

Lo Greco has mixed in great company having been in with Errol Spence and Shawn Porter, and ‘The Italian Sensation’ will come face-to-face with Khan at a press conference in Liverpool tomorrow.

“I wanted people to see the real me. Then I wanted to get back boxing — make sure I come back with a bang.

“The only way to do that is with someone established. I know for a fact he can get me the big fights.
